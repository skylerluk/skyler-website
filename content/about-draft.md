I'm Skyler. I'm a senior at UC Berkeley studying Business (Haas) and Data Science, and I spend most of my time building things and trying to understand myself well enough to build the right ones.

I've come to believe that peace comes before fulfillment, and self-understanding comes before both. So I write — journals for years now, and essays when a thought finally holds still long enough. And I build: an AI CRM, a health tracker, small tools that started as questions I couldn't stop asking. Consulting taught me how deals and teams actually work; building taught me what I actually want to make.

The throughline, if there is one, is a suspicion of the performed self and a pull toward the real one. This site is my attempt at the real one — a record of a life, kept honestly.
