Not all days are made equal. Not all mornings do we wake up imbued with purpose. Every day is difficult. Some days you feel the purpose of life calling out to you. For me it goads me on, saying "catch me if you can."

At times I'm haunted by my potential. In other stages I'm happily grasping it. These days I've swung back and forth. These days I don't feel as stable. That's okay.

See the beauty in the present moment. In what is enough. I am thankful that I am living here. I am grateful for the life that I have. It's quite beautiful.
