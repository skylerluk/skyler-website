What have I learned in the summer of 2024?

Actions speak louder than words. You must follow through when it gets difficult — the reward comes after difficulty. You are not immortal; you must actively consider your current mental and physical health. Rest and listen to your body. Think critically and deeply. Act with intention and start with haste. The outside of your comfort zone is scary, but it can be seen as exciting and rewarding.

The most difficult week of my life culminated in an utter breakdown. Do not try to just power through and believe you are tough. You will collapse. Be kind and realistic to yourself.
