Not a fan of social media. Those platforms are seriously powerful. They can take a user and turn off all reasoning centers. A user is placed in a trance the second they enter — dopamine hit after hit. Mind control powers are very real, and they live among us.

The mask I wore was twice as wide.
It was me I saw again,
for it was always me in the end.

Second night in New York. I'm a bit disappointed — maybe frustrated is the better word. I've slowly lost the excitement and lust for life. It seems completely because I've lost my purpose, and with it, my sense of everything else.
