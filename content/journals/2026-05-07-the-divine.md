Didn't think I'd be able to do a solo trip and enjoy it. Surprised myself. It was immensely enjoyable and beneficial for me. It gave me new insight and understanding of myself.

Ideas are cheap. Execution is all that matters.

There are certain truly special moments — moments in which you encounter the divine. That layer that exists above reality, that breathes color into life. Encountering the divine gives you a chance to shine a flashlight in this room of the mind. You can briefly see what your soul is made of. Its depth, its texture and color. That must be worth everything.
