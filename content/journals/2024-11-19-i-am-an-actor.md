I am the captain of my destiny. I will not spend my time pursuing something I have zero interest in. I will not accept a path that I know is wrong.

To be an artist. To be you. I feel discontent, because I know that I am not me. I know that I am an actor. I'm living another's life. This place is suffocating — it suffocates originality, tells me to fit into a character, a mold. I have to get out.
