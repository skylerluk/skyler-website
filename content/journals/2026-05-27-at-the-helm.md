I define this narrative. I define what this means for me.

The days fly by, as surely as they do the years. Each moment, minute by minute. The test — what will you do in each moment? For they ultimately define your life. Find your why and push through. Leave nothing on the table, absolutely no regrets. Live, live, live and go for everything.

Success on my terms is building something that I am proud of. This life is mine to drive and mine alone. I hold the reins and the ship's wheel. Wind and seas be damned, I am the one at the helm.
