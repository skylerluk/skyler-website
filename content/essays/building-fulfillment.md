Think of the mind as a grand mansion or castle, befitting of spending one's lifetime building. If built correctly, this structure can withstand violent storms or attacks from the outside — and it houses your greatest treasure: peace.

The path toward fulfillment begins with self-understanding. Without a dedicated effort to understand your story, you cannot answer what it is that fulfills you. When I asked myself what actually brings me energy, I arrived at three pillars: working toward something great, relationships with people that matter, and health.

Energy is a finite, daily-renewing currency. Guard it — against stressors, against social media, against draining people.

And balance the pillars patiently. Repeated actions over time make a greater difference than a singular concerted push over several days. To work tirelessly toward a goal is unsustainable. Daily action that incrementally compounds will sustainably lead toward what you're after.
